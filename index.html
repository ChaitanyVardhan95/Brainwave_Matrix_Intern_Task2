Our Chocolate Story

Founded in 1985, Luxury Chocolatier began as a small family-owned patisserie in Brussels, Belgium. Our founder, Jean-Luc Moreau, was passionate about creating the finest chocolate experiences using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Today, we continue this legacy by sourcing the highest quality cocoa beans from sustainable farms around the world. Each chocolate piece is still handcrafted by our master chocolatiers with the same attention to detail and love.

We believe luxury chocolate should be an experience for all senses - from the exquisite packaging to the first melt on your tongue.

Jean-Luc Moreau
